Crystal Quest


Your quest is to select the crystals that fulfill the prophecy below. Each stone has a different worth. Clicking on the stone adds treasure to your treasure chest.

Completing the quest requires that your total treasure matches the treasure set forth in the prophecy. Should your tresure exceed the prophecy number then you have failed. Choose wisely.

Each quest is different, and the worth of the stones change with every quest.
